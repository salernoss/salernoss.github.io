European Union



Digital technologies, safety and sustainability






The declaration highlights the role of digital technologies in fostering democratic participation and pluralistic public debate. It stresses the need for a diverse and multilingual online environment, ownership transparency, protection against disinformation, and access to culturally diverse digital content.

EDRP underscores the significance of safety, security, and privacy in digital technologies. It commits to protecting individuals, businesses, and public services from cybercrime while ensuring effective control over personal and non-personal data, particularly for children and young people.

Finally, the declaration emphasizes the interplay between digital and green transitions. It calls for responsible design, production, and disposal of digital products and services to minimize their environmental and societal impact, alongside providing clearer information on the environmental footprint and energy consumption of such services.
