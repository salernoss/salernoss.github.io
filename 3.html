Main topics of the EDRP







The EDRP revolves around six key themes:

• Placing people and their rights at the core of the digital transformation.
• Supporting solidarity and inclusion to ensure that no one is left behind.
• Upholding freedom of choice online, including transparency in algorithms and AI systems.
• Fostering participation in the digital public space and promoting pluralistic public debate.
• Enhancing safety, security, and empowerment for individuals, with a focus on privacy protection and combating cybercrime.
• Promoting the sustainability of the digital future, considering environmental and societal impact.



The signatories of the declaration commit to ensuring that the digital transformation benefits all individuals residing in the EU, both online and offline. They will take measures to protect and respect digital rights while promoting this approach domestically and internationally.
