European Digital Rights and Principles






European Digital Rights and Principles (EDRP) is a declaration aimed at promoting a European digital transition guided by European values. It proposes a set of digital rights and principles that reflect EU values and foster a sustainable and human-centric approach to the digital transformation.

The declaration, signed by the Presidents of the European Commission, European Parliament, and the Council, demonstrates the shared commitment of the EU and its Member States to implement these rights and principles across all aspects of digital life. It complements existing rights such as data protection, ePrivacy, and the Charter of Fundamental Rights, while drawing from the experience of the European Pillar of Social Rights.

The EDRP provides guidance to the EU and its Member States as they adapt to the digital transformation.
