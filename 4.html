Inclusiveness, informed choices and participation







The declaration emphasizes inclusivity by addressing the digital needs of elderly individuals, those living in rural areas, persons with disabilities, and marginalized or disenfranchised populations. It covers areas such as connectivity, digital education and skills, fair working conditions, and digital public services.

EDRP also emphasizes the importance of informed choices online, including interactions with artificial intelligence and algorithms. It promotes ethical and human-centric AI systems and advocates for transparency in algorithmic use.
